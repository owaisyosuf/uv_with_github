1. **The City of Wrestlers:** Gujranwala has a rich history and tradition of wrestling, producing many renowned wrestlers throughout its history.  It's almost legendary for its wrestling prowess!

2. **A Sweet Legacy:**  Gujranwala is known for its delicious sweets, particularly *ghee ki mithai* (sweet treats made with clarified butter).  These are a local specialty and a must-try for visitors.

3. **Industrial Heartbeat:** Gujranwala plays a significant role in Pakistan's industrial sector, particularly in manufacturing sports goods.  Many of the cricket bats and other sports equipment you see around the world might have originated from factories in Gujranwala.
